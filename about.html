Our Story

MediDeliver was founded in 2020 with a simple vision: to make healthcare accessible, affordable, and convenient for everyone in Uganda.

What began as a small pharmacy delivery service in Kampala has grown into Uganda's most trusted online healthcare platform, serving thousands of customers across the country.

During the COVID-19 pandemic, we recognized the urgent need for contactless medicine delivery. This experience shaped our commitment to innovation and customer safety.

Mr. Zziwa Hope Founder & CEO
